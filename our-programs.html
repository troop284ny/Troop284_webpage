Our Programs

Every Month Troop 284 has an event, in addition to the weekly meetings. We camp multiple times a year, but we also take trips around America, go white water rafting, and even fly planes. Needless to say, your child will never have a boring weekend.

Fun and educational is our goal, and we cater all our activities towards educating scouts on valuable skills such as cooking or basic first aid. In addition, we guide them thoroughly through the ranks toward receiving the coveted Eagle Scout Award.
